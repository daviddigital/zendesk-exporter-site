Zendesk Exporter for Google Sheets

Zendesk has no scheduled ticket export. This add-on pulls any view or search into a Google Sheet — custom fields included — and refreshes it hourly, daily, or weekly.

Install from Google Workspace Marketplace

What it does

Views & searches

Import any Zendesk view, or any search using Zendesk's own syntax — no 1,000-result cap.

The columns you want

Standard fields plus your custom ticket fields. Assignee and requester IDs resolve to real names.

Scheduled refresh

Manual, hourly, daily, or weekly. Each tab is stamped with its last-refreshed time — even with the sheet closed.

Private by design

Credentials stay in your Google account's add-on storage. Ticket data goes straight from Zendesk to your sheet — we run no servers.
